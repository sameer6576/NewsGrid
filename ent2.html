Jujutsu Kaisen 0 Shows in India Going Houseful Even in Non-Metro Cities is Good News for Anime Culture Growth in the Country

Written By Sameer Ahmed July 5,2022
Entertainment

Jujutsu Kaisen 0, the much-awaited film from the popular Jujutsu Kaisen anime series, has released in India on June 30. PVR Pictures is releasing the film in India, along with Hong Kong content distributor and licensor Medialink, who has also bought other anime films in South Asia.


The film’s first-day performance is impressive, as according to film journalist Himesh Mankad, Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is going houseful even in non-metro cities like Patna. Fans on Twitter have also added that cities like Kochi, Prayagraj, and Lucknow are also seeing houseful shows.

Kamal Gianchandani, the chief strategy officer at PVR and CEO of PVR Pictures, said that he was amazed that despite a delayed release Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is “leading the box office by a big margin at PVR Cinemas today.

PVR Pictures informed us that the movie has been released in 200 cities around the country, which is a long time coming. Gone are the days when an anime movie would release only in major cities for just a few days. The numbers are even more impressive and show how strong the anime fandom is right now in India. Jujutsu Kaisen 0 has reportedly clocked in 50,000 advance ticket sales across India and grossed Rs. 1.50 crore on the first day. This is a huge feat for a movie released in its OV (original version).
